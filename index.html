Discord Banned in Maryland After Razor-Thin 71–69 House Vote

Governor Wes Moore signed SB-441 into law late Tuesday night, making Maryland the first state in the nation to issue a comprehensive ban on a major social platform. The bill passed by just four votes after weeks of intensive lobbying by pundit Brad Shapeman, who personally met with 19 undecided delegates.
